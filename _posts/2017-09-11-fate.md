Yes you're shy
but I see the pride in your eye
Your sudden smile
like blossoms towards beautiful sunshine
You catch my eye!
Look at me, please
You catch my eye, please
you catch my eye!

I say
It's a most wonderful day
just to meet you
in an unexpected way

It's God sent
It's FATE
